THE HALLS ARE HUNGRY

Escape From School And Death

You’re already trying to survive school, now try surviving a zombie infected outbreak too. Your regular school day became a disaster when a sudden outburst of commotion came in the halls, talking about a zombie invasion, and you have a feeling on knowing who caused it all.

Your goal? To get out immediately.

The problem? There's seven floors, and only one exit.

As you descend to each floor level, you find zombies that are stronger, scarier, and familiar looking faces in a new and unfamiliar looking appearance...

How will you fight back? How will you escape? How did this all happen? You thought school couldn’t get any worse, and yet, you are now reaching...

WHAT IS 7TH HELL?

Want to know a summary before playing our game?

We got you!

It’s a normal school day for you, starting off with your classes and getting through your day as per usual.

In that early morning, a well known classmate for his notorious behavior, was in the lab creating something new.

The school speakers goes off, warning students that there was an outbreak quickly spreading to the school.

Your task is to escape and survive by defending yourself by descending down to the exit while getting weapons.

Quickly get out of the school safely, get rid of the problem that started it all, and be prepared to go through hell.
